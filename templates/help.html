How to use

Welcome to Joke Bot

Getting Started
  • Choose a mood from the main screen to receive a random joke
  • Rate the joke with 👍 or 👎 — ratings affect future selections
  • Press 🔄 to skip the current joke and get a new one
  • Press 🔗 to copy a shareable link to the current joke
Rating System

Jokes with higher ratings appear more frequently. Poorly rated jokes still have a small chance of showing up — nothing is ever completely removed.

Your vote is counted once per joke, and the rating is shared with all users.

Sharing Jokes

Press the 🔗 button to copy a permanent link. Anyone opening the link will see the same joke.
